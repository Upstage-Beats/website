About

UpstageBeats is the passion project of two Canadian producers, Uno and Ancrnotokay. Aiming to provide artists with easy access to a selection of premium, hand-picked beats that fit all styles, the team at UpstageBeats is dedicated to helping you create the perfect track.

Unlimited MP3 and WAV leases are currently available for purchase on the store (see Legal page for more info). If you are interested in purchasing exclusive rights or track outs to any of the beats listed, please contact us using the form below or by email at upstage.beats@gmail.com to discuss terms of purchase.
